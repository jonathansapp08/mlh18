Surveys Charities Profile

Mission Statement

Servey is an aggregator of online surveys that reward users like you with money upon completion. Servey takes the money you earn from these surveys and donates it to a charity of your choice. We offer a wide range of options for our users, both in the type of surveys available and the charities we are partnered with.



Get Started

Servey is a non-profit business, so 100% of the money you earn is donated to the charity or charities of your choice. After creating a profile, select your favorite charity or charities. You can access your favorites easily before completing a survey, or choose from any of our other available charities. Servey makes charitable contributions fun and easy, and saves your information so you can see how much total money you've raised for charity on your profile. Get started now!
